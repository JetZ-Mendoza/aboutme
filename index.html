MCT Hotel Booking

An application that enables the user to book a room and calculates the fee automatically when checking out. The cashier can select their clients desired room type, check-in and check-out dates, and any additional services. The system computes the total cost based on the duration of stay, room rates, and extra charges. Payments can be processed through various methods, including credit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. The application provides booking confirmations and receipts for transparency.
